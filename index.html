Coffee Types

Iced Coffee

Iced coffee is available outside of Japan, but they tend to be a sweeter drink as many come pre-made with milk and sugar, or sometimes even ice cream.

Coffee Fresh

When you order a cup of coffee in Japan, you will be asked if you want milk and sugar. Please be aware that “milk” is sometimes different from what you get when you order coffee in your country.

Doppio

A Doppio is a double shot of espresso with no added hot water or milk. This results in 60 ml of coffee. Doppio means 'double' in Japan.

Short Macchiato

A short macchiato is served in a small glass consisting of a single espresso shot then filled with creamy steamed milk and finished with a small layer of foam.
